1000-Introduction


INTRODUCTION TO SYMPLAY

SymPlay is a video player, which allows you to stream videos of any length from your hard disc or any other mass storage media. It supports any kind of resolution and data rate (frames per second). As it is completely independed from the I/O and CPU speed of your hardware, you will just have different playback quality.

You can download "VID" video files playable with SymPlay and live videos, showing SymPlay in action, in the download area.

To produce your own SymPlay videos, please download SymStudio, which is the Windows XP tool for creating applications and videos for SymbOS.

1100-Feature overview


FEATURES

What can you do with SymPlay
SymPlay is the first and only CPC application, which allows you to play videos from your hard disc or compact flash card. It supports any kind of resolution and data rate (frames per second).As it is completely independed from the hardware, it behaves on a 12MHz TREX like on an original 4MHz CPC just with different playback quality. You can download "VID" video files playable with SymPlay and live videos, showing SymPlay in action, in the download area on www.symbos.de To produce your own SymPlay videos, please download Unify,which is the Windows tool for creating applications and videos for SymbOS.

2100-Keyboard shortcuts


KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

The following is a list of all available keyboard shortcuts.

Return or C play/pause video
Z jump to the beginning
X skip 5 seconds backward
V skip 5 seconds forward
B jump to the end
Ctrl+O open new video
Ctrl+P open option dialogue
